Fortunate by chance alone cannot ensure this state indefinitely. Luck inevitably runs out, and one must rely on oneself in continue functioning. Therefore, you should rely on yourself and ignite the fire and desire within your soul, rather than seeking external validatation and emotional support from others.

Some people may win a lottery, have a successful career, but without building a solid foundation, learning dogmata about life, and relying on their own abilities and instincts, they have a high likelihood of losing this state. This is because they lack the skills and knowledge to handle life's challenges.

Additionally, this is why you should avoid focusing only on one aspect of life (i.e. studying), and, instead, take steps back to explore different things and gain a broader understanding of the current meta. By doing so and living through tough life situations people develop the ability to live rather than perform a specific task.
